The page is called Hot-Stuff and it`s nothing at all fancy. I didn`t know at the time that I could persue this passion as a career and unfortunetly my life journey headed in a different direction and I persued a career in the hospitality sector because I enjoyed dealing with people.
I decided at the end of last year 2016 that my true-calling wasn`t in hospitality anymore and that I needed to figure out what it was.
Someone sent me a link on facebook to Shaw Academy and I decided to sign up and do some courses but had no idea on what to study
I then remembered about that page I built several years ago so I started with the introduction to web development course and haven`t looked back.
I`m passionate about building awesome interactive, clean and repsonsive web applications and enjoy learning new things about the web each day.
